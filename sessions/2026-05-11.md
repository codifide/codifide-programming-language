## What shipped

   outer parser now assembles physical lines into a single

   `post`, `when`, and bind right-hand sides. EOF-safe peeks in
   `expr_parser.parse_atom`. Spec update in `docs/LANGUAGE.md §Line
   continuation`. Closes AUD-2026-05-11-01 (P1).

2. **Polymorphic `reverse`.** `reverse` now accepts both strings
   and lists. Capability manifest records `returns: "Any"` for it.
   Spec update in `docs/LANGUAGE.md §Primitive polymorphism`
   establishes the rule.

3. **Hint messages.** The lexer's `%` error points at `mod`. The
   interpreter's `unknown callable` and `unbound name` errors
   include one-line hints for `str.reverse`, `clock.hour`,
   `str.upper`, and siblings. Closes AUD-2026-05-11-02 (P2).

4. **`docs/AGENT_QUICKREF.md`.** One-page distillation of the
   primitive surface and the common-guess pitfalls. Linked from
   `README.md` and `docs/FOR_AGENTS.md`.

5. **Regression tests.** Gemini's failing fixtures plus hint-fire
   tests. Seven new tests; suite at 129/129.
